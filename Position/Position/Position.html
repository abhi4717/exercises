Static positioned image

  • This is the default position of an object.
  • Any alignment has no effect. The image on left is padded by 150px and from top by 100px.

Absolute positioned image

  • In absolute positioned image, object is positioned with respect to position of its parent.
  • The image is right and top padded by 0px.

Relative positioned image

  • Relative positioned image will be positioned with respect to its initial position
  • The image is positioned to left by -150px and top by 0px

Fixed positioned Image

  • Fixed positioned image will positioned with respect to the screen
  • The image is positioned to right by 15px and bottom by 10px.
